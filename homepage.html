About Sri Lanka

The Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka,is an island situated in the Indian ocean. The capital city of Sri Lanka is Sri Jayawardhana Pura Kotte. The endless beaches, waterfalls, timeless ruins, welcoming people, oodles of elephants, surfing, cheap prices, fun trains, famous tea and flavourful food make Sri Lanka irresistible.
